# Integrations

Kravn connects to the tools your teams already use and **re-exposes them under your own governance** —
SSO/RBAC, per-team entitlements, redaction and audit — with nothing leaving your perimeter. Two kinds,
browsed together in the operator's **Catalog**:

- **Built-in integrations** run in-process inside Kravn (app-only credentials, encrypted at rest) — enable
  one, enter credentials once, and its tools join your registry.
- **Catalog** — a curated set of public **MCP servers** you add in one click. No-auth and API-key servers
  connect immediately; **OAuth 2.1** servers sign in with the provider (Kravn runs the full flow and stores
  tokens encrypted).

Either way, their tools flow into the same team-governed **MCP endpoints** as everything else — you decide
who can use what, and every call is policy-checked and audited.
